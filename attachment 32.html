The Small Steps to Greatness

"Hi, welcome! Today, I want to tell you a story about Mia..."

Mia dreamed of speaking confidently in front of a crowd.

But... every time she tried, her voice shook. Her hands trembled. Her words stumbled.

So, what did she do? She started small. She spoke in front of a mirror. One sentence... then two... then three. Every morning. Every evening.

Slowly... her voice became stronger. Her words flowed. Her confidence grew... step by step.

And then... came the day of her first speech. Her heart raced, but she remembered all her practice. She spoke clearly. She smiled. She connected with the audience.

And she realized... greatness is built on small steps, repeated with courage.

So, here's the lesson: never underestimate your small efforts.

Speak slowly. Speak clearly. Speak with confidence.

Every word you say brings you closer to the voice you dream of.
